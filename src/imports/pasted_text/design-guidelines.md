Analyze the current website design and significantly improve the visual style.

Your goal is to refine the color palette, gradients, and visual elements so the website feels like it was designed by a professional product designer.

The design should remain clean and minimal but visually interesting and memorable.

--------------------------------

COLOR PALETTE

Create a refined and modern color palette suitable for a technology website.

Use a light design with:

– white or very light background
– soft neutral tones
– a primary accent color
– a secondary accent color.

Colors should feel elegant and professional.

Avoid overly saturated colors.

--------------------------------

GRADIENTS

Introduce subtle modern gradients.

Gradients should be soft and elegant, not loud.

Examples:

– soft blue → violet gradients
– light blue → cyan gradients
– subtle pastel gradients
– gradient highlights behind sections.

Gradients should add depth and modern visual interest.

--------------------------------

VISUAL DESIGN ELEMENTS

Add subtle design elements that make the layout feel more sophisticated.

Examples:

– abstract shapes
– soft geometric forms
– gradient light blobs
– thin system lines
– subtle grid backgrounds
– soft glow accents.

These elements should remain very subtle.

--------------------------------

SECTION TRANSITIONS

Make transitions between sections visually interesting.

Sections should feel connected rather than separated.

Use techniques such as:

– soft gradient transitions
– overlapping shapes
– diagonal section separators
– layered backgrounds
– flowing design elements.

However, the design must remain clean and structured.

Avoid heavy decorative elements.

--------------------------------

DEPTH AND LAYERS

Introduce visual depth using:

– layered cards
– soft shadows
– subtle background gradients
– light glass-like surfaces.

--------------------------------

OVERALL STYLE

The final result should feel:

– modern
– elegant
– minimal
– technological
– visually refined.

The design should look comparable to modern technology websites and premium SaaS products.

Think like a senior product designer and refine the visual identity carefully.

Maintain simplicity while making the design visually distinctive.
